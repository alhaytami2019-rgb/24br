What does an AI engineer do?

An AI engineer designs, builds, and deploys artificial intelligence systems that solve real-world problems. This includes creating AI agents, building multi-agent systems, fine-tuning language models, and integrating AI capabilities into production software applications. Unlike traditional software engineers, AI engineers focus on systems that can reason, plan, and take actions autonomously.
